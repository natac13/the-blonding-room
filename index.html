About Us

The Blonding Room is a full service salon specializing in, not only blondes, but all colour techniques. Picking a name for the salon was one of the hardest parts, but don't worry, we welcome all hair tones, not just blondes 😉

The Blonding Room came to light after 2 best friends decided one day they would work together again!

Our main goal was to create a space where it never feels like a day of work, just a couple of friends hanging out, whicle doing some beautiful hair styling.

Our dream is to have a room full of independant aftist, all working as a team to achieve that goal.
